Bachelor of Vocation (B.Voc) in 3D Animation & Visual Effects

The B.Voc in 3D Animation & Visual Effects is an industry-aligned program developed in collaboration with MAAC (Maya Academy of Advanced Creativity), aimed at equipping students with advanced creative and technical skills in animation, filmmaking, and visual storytelling.

The six-semester curriculum blends foundational design thinking with cutting-edge technologies like Generative AI, interactive 3D, and real-time rendering, preparing students for the evolving media and entertainment industry.

Early semesters cover core areas such as Digital Content & Design with GenAI, Pre-Production, Filmmaking, and Photography, along with workplace-readiness modules like Health & Safety. As students progress, they dive into Motion Design, Video & Audio Editing with GenAI, and complete their first creative project.

Advanced semesters focus on 3D Animation Essentials, CG Asset Modeling, Digital Sculpting, and Character Setup. The program also integrates Communication Skills, Organizational Behavior, and Entrepreneurship, building holistic career readiness.

Later modules include Look & Feel Development, Interactive 3D, Tech Animation, FX Fundamentals, and 3D Camera Tracking, leading up to capstone projects and training in Advanced Compositing and IPR. With a strong balance of theory and hands-on experience, students graduate with an industry-ready portfolio and skill set for careers in animation, VFX, game design, and film production.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a B.Voc. in 3D Animation & VFX?
A 3-year undergraduate (UGC-recognized) degree granting advanced skills in 3D animation, visual effects, and real-time project workflows
Which board/university awards this degree?
Scope Global Skills University, in collaboration with MAAC's industry curriculum, awards the B.Voc. in 3D Animation & VFX.
Do I need a specific background to apply?
No. Any 10+2 pass-out (all streams) with 45%+ aggregate is eligible. Freshers, diploma holders, and career-changers are welcome.
What software and tools will I learn?
You'll train on industry-standard tools: Autodesk Maya, Blender, Nuke, After Effects, Houdini, Marvelous Designer, Unreal Engine, and more.
Is placement guaranteed?
We provide 100% placement assistance via tie-ups with studios (Technicolor, DNEG, Red Chillies VFX) and game companies (EA, Ubisoft). Final placement depends on your portfolio and interviews.
How do I claim the "Free Laptop" offer?
Join for a career program & pay your complete fee in 6 months from your date of registration
Can I do an internship during the degree?
Yes, in Semesters 5 & 6 you'll work on live industry projects/internships with our partner studios.
